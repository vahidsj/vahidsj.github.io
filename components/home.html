RESEARCH AREA

My research centers on bridging the gap between conversational information-seeking, personalization, and evaluation, with the long-term goal of enabling machines to exhibit human-like communication, subjective reasoning, and commonsense knowledge.

First, I investigate how conversational AI systems can better understand and incorporate users‚Äô preferences and opinions. This involves designing systems that engage in genuinely personalized dialogue and deliver contextually relevant information tailored to individual users.

Second, I explore the role of storytelling and narrative coherence in conversational AI. By integrating narrative techniques, I aim to make interactions more engaging, comprehensible, and applicable to real-world domains such as education and interactive learning.

Finally, I focus on evaluation, developing and comparing new methods that combine automatic metrics with gamification-based approaches. My goal is to establish more robust and reliable tools for assessing conversational systems.

Here are my core research areas:

  • - Human-Computer Interaction
  • - Conversational Information-Seeking
  • - Narrative Understanding and Generation
  • - Computational Social Psychology
  • - Evaluation for Conversational Systems
